Project context:
“Белпомощник” is a personal digital civic assistant for citizens of Belarus. It helps people solve life situations step by step. Users can search for a problem, get a clear action plan, save checklists, track documents, receive reminders, follow relevant legal updates, and understand where to go and what documents are needed.

The product must feel like it was created by a dominant multi-billion-dollar technology corporation: iconic, expensive, extremely polished, confident, simple, scalable, and trustworthy.

* Apple-level clarity

* a student project
